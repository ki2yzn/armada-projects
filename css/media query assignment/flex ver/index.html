Pikachu

Pikachu, the Mouse Pokémon, is an Electric-type creature known for its ability to generate electricity, which it discharges through its cheeks.

Bulbasaur

Bulbasaur, the Seed Pokémon, is a Grass type Pokemon that carries a plant bulb on its back, which absorbs sunlight to help it grow.

Gengar

Gengar, the Shadow Pokémon, is a Ghost and Poison-type creature known for its mischievous nature and ability to hide in shadows.
